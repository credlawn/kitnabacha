Ledgeo

Privacy Policy · Last updated: July 2026

Ledgeo is a personal finance tracking app. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it — simply and transparently.

What We Collect

✅ Account Info — When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and name. This is used only to identify your account.

✅ Your Financial Data — Transactions, contacts, and expense records you enter are stored on our server. We keep this data solely to provide backup and sync across your devices.

✅ Crash Reports — Anonymous crash data (via Sentry) helps us fix bugs. No personal or financial data is included.

What We Do NOT Do

✕ Sell your data — ever.

✕ Share with third parties.

✕ Analyze your spending or transactions.

✕ Use your data for advertising.

Why We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on our server for two simple reasons:

  • Backup — so you never lose your records if you switch devices.
  • Multi-device sync — so you can access the same data from any device.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on a secure server in India (Mumbai). All communication between the app and server is encrypted using HTTPS. We do not read or access your financial data beyond what is necessary to run the service.

Your Control

You are in full control of your data:

  • Delete any transaction or contact anytime from within the app.
  • Delete your entire account from the app — this permanently removes all your data from our server.
